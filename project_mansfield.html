Mansfield 16 units in West Adams within steps of the metro.

Development

Midos, as part of an exciting new partnership, has plans for a new 16 unit apartment building at 2929 S Mansfield Avenue Los Angeles. Located on a 7,215 SQFT lot, the approximately 15,000 SQFT building will consist of all 2 bedroom units.

The property is located in West Adams, a very walkable neighborhood with immediate access to mass transit and directly adjacent to Culver City, where thousands of renters work at Netflix, Apple, and more high paying tech jobs. Nestled in a great pocket of West Adams, this particular property will have access to lots of unique new retail shops and developments.

Because Mansfield is located within 750 feet of the metro station at La Brea/Exposition, the property qualifies for tier 3 TOC designation, which allows for increased density and additional incentives.

The building will offer the latest new technology and amenities, including a rooftop with 360 views of the city.
