The
team

David Hirsch

Co-Founder and CEO

As CEO, David is responsible for managing the strategies, direction and operations at MIDOS Development. David is a licensed General Contractor in California, as well as in 3 additional states. David started his career in media and advertising, with a focus on the remodeling and construction industry. He co-founded 1800Remodel.com, a national home improvement brand and platform, to allow homeowners to connect with local contractors.

Over the past 10 years, David has personally hired and trained over 500 employees, has accumulated expertise in general construction, renovation and development, and has personally overseen completion on over 3,000+ projects, valued at from $80M to $250M.

Joseph Fine

Co-Founder and Director of Operations

Joseph has been involved in real estate and construction since his teens, becoming a contractor, underwriter and real estate broker. He has extensive experience in operating a wide variety of large scale construction, renovation and development initiatives from start to completion, bringing his personal brand of obsessive attention to detail to every project. In addition to his intimate involvement with all planned and ongoing construction projects, Joseph is the primary systems architect behind most of the unique processes utilized daily by the MIDOS team, where his distinctive foresight, calculated thoroughness and accuracy allows complex projects to progress smoothly.

Chrisopher Welch

CFO

Chris is a seasoned finance executive with more than 20 years as CFO, COO or principal in 5 different companies. With tenure working in retail food & beverage, technology, automotive services and real estate development, Chris brings a wide range of experience to MID Construction/MIDOS Development. Chris manages all back office, finance, administration, IT and treasury functions for the company. Chris has a BA in Architecture from UC Berkeley, an MBA from UCLA, is a licensed general contractor (874402) and also serves as a Design Review Board member for the City of Glendale, CA.

Jake Heller

Director of Development

Jake is currently Director of Development for Midos Development Co. He Graduated from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University with a double major in Finance and Real Estate and shortly after began his career as a property manager and broker. At Marcus & Millichap, Jake was awarded the Pace Setter award, which is only given to agents who perform at the highest level during their first four months with the company.

J Jake also spent time working at the family business, Heller Properties, where he played an integral role in securing entitlements for a new 40 unit development project on the Los Angeles/Beverly Hills border. Prior to that, he managed the design team through completion on a 130 unit apartment building in Henderson, NV.

Today, Jake and Midos Development Co are developing 94 units with a total capitalization cost of $34 Million and over 90,000 square feet of gross buildable space.

Steve Ghermazian

Head of Construction

Steve has 20+ years of Operations and Management experience, where he has directly managed an extensive variety of construction projects, hiring and negotiating with designers, architects, engineers, subcontractors, and vendors. Steve’s hands-on involvement includes overseeing virtually every facet of ongoing construction, as varied as onboarding and negotiations, managing project timelines, schedules, compliance with building and safety code, permitting, training, and virtually anything else related to day to day construction management.

Taranjeet Chawla

Accounting Manager

Taranjeet is an experienced accounting manager with a demonstrated history of working in the construction industry. Skilled in Analytical Skills, Management, Management Information Systems (MIS), Payroll, and Marketing Strategy. Strong administrative professional with a PGDBA focused in Human Resource & Finance from Symbiosis Centre of Distance Learning, Pune.

Seyed Safavian

Director of Engineering

Seyed holds two degrees in Engineering, and has 20 years of architectural and structural expertise with residential, commercial, and multifamily development projects. Seyed heads up MIDOS architectural review, structural engineering and calculations, project management, structural observation reporting and more.

Gabe Hirsch

Marketing Director

Gabe has extensive experience in various forms of digital marketing, having been involved in a wide variety of media and advertising ventures.

Joshua Berman

Head of Investor Relations

Coming to Investor Relations from the world of personal and business finance, Joshua is securities and insurance licensed and has specialized in alternative investments and non-correlated asset classes for the diversification of client portfolios as a tool to preserve and grow wealth.

Prior to working with securities, Joshua was a management and business consultant, focusing on developing start-ups as well as problem-solving for small businesses and divisions of larger companies. Originally from Philadelphia, he received bachelor’s degrees from both Columbia University and the Jewish Theological Seminary and earned his EMBA from where he specialized in Finance and Strategic Growth.

Joshua has served on several boards and civic organizations such as the Benedict Canyon Association, C-PAB for the LAPD West Bureau, Bel Air Beverly Crest Neighborhood Council, and the Columbia Alumni Association.
