To the project website »

Redesign is a new home-design center that will be constructed at the Ikea complex in Kiryat Ata, near highway 22. The center will provide an inspiring home décor experience by offering 20,000 sq. m. of leading home-design brand names, and design solutions for all purposes, while setting new standards for this field in Israel.

The center will also feature REDLIFESTYLE, which will offer entertainment options and food courts, as well as REDLAB, a hub specifically designed for professionals, designers and architects that will host innovative exhibitions, product launches and inspiring lectures.
